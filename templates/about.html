About the Science Club

History and Founders

The Science Club was founded in 2005 by Dr. Dan Wu, with the vision to inspire young minds in the field of science.

Mission, Vision, and Goals

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to explore their scientific curiosity and creativity.

Our vision is to be a leading club in fostering innovative scientific projects and nurturing future scientists.

Our goals include promoting scientific knowledge, supporting innovative projects, and recognizing student achievements.

Innovations and Awards

  1. 2010: Introduced the Annual Science Fair
  2. 2012: Won the National Science Club Award
  3. 2015: Launched the Innovation Hub
  4. 2018: Received the Excellence in Education Award
  5. 2021: Celebrated 15 years of scientific achievements
